# 🧠 AI-Powered Business OS
### A Claude-native virtual team for solopreneurs who run real operations

---

## What is this?

Running a business alone doesn't mean running it without support.

This repository is a collection of Claude-native AI agents designed to cover every critical business function a solopreneur needs — from strategic decisions to client communication, from project management to sales. Together, they form an **operational ecosystem**: a virtual team you can deploy on demand.

Built and battle-tested at **Disrupcom**. Adapted for clients across industries.

---

## The Problem This Solves

When you're a solopreneur, you're the CEO, the ops lead, the sales rep, the designer, and the client manager — all at once. Context-switching kills productivity. Inconsistency kills quality. Doing everything yourself doesn't scale.

Generic AI prompts don't solve this. A one-liner prompt gives you a generic answer. These agents give you a **role-aware collaborator** that understands its function, speaks in the right voice, and adapts to your business context.

## How It Works

Each agent is structured as a Claude system prompt with three core layers:

1. **Role definition** — who this agent is and what they own
2. **Behavioral guidelines** — how they think, communicate, and prioritize
3. **Adaptability variables** — fields you fill in to make the agent work for *your* specific business

This means the same agent that runs operations at Disrupcom can be redeployed for a consulting firm, a creative agency, or a service-based startup — with minimal configuration.
